[ The village ]

Somewhere warm to land.

A pedestrian village at 1,600 m — larch, stone and woodsmoke, twelve minutes from the last chair to a hot drink. Four things you'll need before and after.

The Cornice Lodge

Forty rooms and a south-facing sauna deck, ski-in to a boot room that dries everything by dawn. Half-board, because you won't want to leave the fire.

from €240 / night · ski-in ski-out

Ski & Board School

Fifty instructors in eleven languages. First-timers to off-piste guiding — small groups, or private if you'd rather not have witnesses. Kids' club runs all day.

group from €55 · private from €190

Après at Le Sérac

The terrace that catches the last sun on the mountain. Vin chaud, a very serious fondue, and a band that starts the moment the Gondola stops. Bring your ski boots; everyone does.

opens 15:00 · fondue from €28

Rental & Repair

Fresh-tuned skis and boards fitted while you drink a coffee, plus overnight edge-and-wax so you're not the one skating on tram-lines. Boot fitters who actually fit boots.

rental from €32 / day · tune €25
